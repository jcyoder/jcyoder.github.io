About Me...

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio page. I am a Colorado native, born in Denver and raised in Arvada. I have spent my entire career working on software development teams in a variety of roles.

Most recently, I have worked in product/program management and as a Scrum Master. I would like to return to a more technical role and transition into Web development. WebLab - A Web Development Boot Camp, FRCC has given me an exciting opportunity to do just that.
