About Me

Hello! Welcome to my portfolio page. I am a Colorado native, born in Denver and raised in Arvada. I came to Northern Colorado in 1990 and earned a Bachelor's in mathematics with an emphasis in computer science. I have spent my entire career working on software development teams in a variety of roles.

Most recently, I have been working as a Web developer and graphic designer. Before this adventure, I worked in product/program management and as a Scrum Master.
